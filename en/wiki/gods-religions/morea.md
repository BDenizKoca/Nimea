# Morea

Domain: Death, Sickness, Endings

Symbol: A withered flower beside a skull; a pale candle held by bone

## Cults

- Bone Weavers; Empty Bell.

## Rituals

- No death passes unnamed; the sick are not abandoned; no fire at rites.
